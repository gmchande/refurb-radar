# Social Post

I open sourced Refurb Radar: a tiny Ruby watcher for Apple Canada refurbished
Mac inventory.

It watches exact configs, confirms direct buyability signals from Apple, and
alerts you fast. No checkout bot. No browser automation. No database. Just Ruby,
JSON, launchd/Kamal, and a small status page.

Useful if you are hunting for a very specific refurb Mac mini, Mac Studio, or
MacBook configuration and do not want noisy "some Mac is back" alerts.

Repo: https://github.com/gmchande/refurb-radar
